Core Boys

  • You start as the Orange one.
  • If a fighter is knocked out of the bottom or sides of the screen, the other fighter gets a point.
  • Controller:
  • Left stick to move.
  • Tap up on the left stick to jump.
  • Y to attack upwards with a spin move.
  • B to attack with a charged shot in the direction you are facing.
  • A to down-spike.
  • X to punch-spike forward and backwards.
  • RB to shield, if it breaks you will be stunned.
  • Keyboard:
  • WAD to move (W to jump or double jump).
  • IJKL to fight.
  • I to attack upwards with a spin move.
  • L to attack with a charged shot in the direction you are facing.
  • K to down-spike.
  • J to punch-spike forward and backwards.
  • O to shield, if it breaks you will be stunned.
